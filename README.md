**RoghanYab** is an advanced car oil guide and vehicle maintenance application that helps users:
- Find the correct engine oil for their vehicle
- View accurate technical specifications
- Log and manage vehicle service history

The app is focused on the Iranian automotive market and provides detailed data such as **recommended oil viscosity, API grade, oil capacity (with and without filter)**, and essential maintenance tips.

RoghanYab is built as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** with offline support and can also be packaged as an **Android APK**.
